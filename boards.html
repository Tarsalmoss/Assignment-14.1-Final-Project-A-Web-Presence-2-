Robotics and Embedded Systems Objectives

  • Design and complete robotic and embedded systems solutions that address real-world situations and challenges.
  • Demonstrate embedded microprocessor system skills such as microcontroller selection, digital logic design, and signal processing.
  • Develop real-time embedded systems using industry-standard tools.
  • Apply knowledge of control systems, sensors, and actuators.
  • Implement secure hardware and software systems.
  • Integrate hardware and software into functional robotic systems.
